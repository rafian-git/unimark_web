SwapnoNeer

A premium gated community approach by the Unimark Holdings Ltd for only 200 number of customers. Yes it’s a luxury to afford about 50% open area within the project to make it more healthy and afford more breathing space. There are always challenges in the development in housing projects but for us we would try to address and resolve more problems that just space crisis in Dhaka.

Goal to leave 50% space within our project helping us solving many crisis as such enhanced air quality, very well managed and prevented noise pollution , more open field for the kids to embrace their childhood with the earth, enough light and air passing through the area, a lake and enough seasonal trees balances the ecology by attracting more birds . It’s the nature we are trying to recall and set it free to embrace us and allow us to live a healthy life. Needless to make you feel the reality we are going through in upbringing our kids while depriving them from letting them feel the green, running across the play field, playing real games than virtual, obstructing their natural growth and letting them growing with obesity and many more. Hence forth here is simple solution to all problems.

SwapnoNeer Housing Project aims to offer every possible solution to make living better in the midst of nature evolves in an outskirt of Dhaka City and at a very closest distance of New Dhaka recognized Purbachal New Town connected through 120 feet proposed highway considered to be built within couple of years from now. You would prefer Swapno Neer Project over many other projects across for the very few reasons out of may as furnished below:

  • Targeted land area is naturally high and doesn’t require much of earth filling work .
  • Location is very strategic and close to Govt Project.
  • Availibility of utilities(Power, Cable and Internet) makes it convenient .
  • PWD Damn and lake run over the project offeres and advantage to pass through the earth water of the project.
  • Lush green surrounding the project area and many more plantation of trees to be done surrounding and across the project roads makes it even better .
  • Resident occupancy will be built on only 50% land and rest 50% land are preserved to be open.
  • Land within the project are owned by private owners . No such govt khaas land is found recorded till date.
  • Offering registration to you after paying 50% of the land price makes your investment less risky and more secured.
  • Layout plan is designed by highly experienced professional firm of the country and who are also committed to devote themselves in all stages of development of the plan.
  • Lake within the project offers you a convenience and allure you to come out of home and take a walk by the clean lake surrounded by green.
  • It’s a blue green city which is now acclaimed as worlds most preferred development format of new cities.
  • All necessary civic amenities as such Mosque, Elementary school, Senior School, Club, Health Centre, Convenient super shop, Clinic, Park, Play field,
  • Wide road network secured by cctv suveilance and watch guards ensures safty of each customers.
  • Semi Smart City to be considered as the exit-entry shall be controlled by smart card
  • Smart card shall also be tapped for all kind of payments of the social services including club membership and shopping credits .
